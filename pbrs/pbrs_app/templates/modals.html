Qualifications to Become a Real Estate Agent

  1. Completion of at least 2 years or 72 units of college education.
  2. Of good moral character who has not been convicted of any crime involving moral turpitude.
  3. At least 12 credit units of training and seminars in real estate brokerage constituting.
